Bhai suno, aaj toh Youth Day India ke baare mein baat karte hain! Yaar, pata hai kya? India mein Youth Day celebrate karna ek bahut hi interesting history se judi hai.

Mazedaar baat yeh hai, originally yeh 1973 mein lagaya gaya tha, agar aapko pata hai toh, India mein youngsters ko youth empowerment ke liye encouraged karne ke liye! Par, 2011 mein, Prime Minister ne isko ek national holiday banaya, aur ab yeh 15 August ko celebrate hota hai.

Aur yeh jaaniye, India mein, 2023 mein, 67% population youth hai! Seriously!  Yeh means India ka future, ye sab youngsters mein hai. Aur, pata hai kya, Youth Day par youngsters saath mein rallies, music shows, aur competitions bhi karte hain.

Socho zara, is din par youngsters ka importance kitna hai. Ye din youngsters ko apni talent ko showcase karne aur apni dreams ko pursue karne ke liye ek mauka hai.
